Welcome to OncoDétect Québec

Your trusted clinic for cancer screening using advanced biomarker detection in blood and urine.

About Us

OncoDétect Québec is dedicated to early cancer detection through non-invasive screening using biomarkers found in body fluids. Our mission is to provide accessible, accurate, and timely cancer screening for everyone in Québec.

Our Technology

We utilize cutting-edge biochemical and molecular analyses to detect cancer biomarkers in blood and urine samples. Our state-of-the-art labs and expert staff ensure reliable results to support early diagnosis and better outcomes.
